# Privacy Policy for TND System Mobile Application

**Last Updated:** October 16, 2025

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to TND System ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our TND System mobile application (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

- **Register an account:**
  - Full name
  - Email address
  - Phone number
  - Employee ID
  - Division/Department
  - Profile photo (optional)

- **Use the App:**
  - Username and password (encrypted)
  - User role and permissions
  - Work assignments and schedules

### 2.2 Visit and Audit Data

When you perform visits or audits using the App, we collect:

- **Visit Information:**
  - Date and time of visit
  - Outlet/location visited
  - Check-in and check-out timestamps
  - Visit duration
  - Visit status (completed, in-progress, scheduled)

- **Checklist Responses:**
  - Checklist answers (OK, Not OK, N/A)
  - Notes and comments
  - Scores and ratings

- **Photos:**
  - Images uploaded during visits
  - Photo metadata (timestamp, file size)
  - Photos are associated with specific checklist items

### 2.3 Location Information

With your permission, we collect location data:

- **GPS Coordinates:**
  - Location when checking in/out of visits
  - Used for visit verification and reporting
  - Collected only during active visits

**Note:** You can disable location access in your device settings, but some features may not work properly.

### 2.4 Device Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device:

- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system version (Android/iOS)
- Unique device identifiers
- App version
- Mobile network information
- IP address

### 2.5 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the App:

- Features accessed
- Time spent in the App
- Visit frequency
- Performance and crash reports
- Error logs

- Send notifications about:
  - New visit assignments
  - Visit reminders
  - System updates
  - Important announcements

### 3.4 Improvement and Development

- Analyze app usage to improve features
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Develop new features
- Enhance user experience

### 4.1 Within Your Organization

  - Monitor compliance metrics

- **System Administrators:**
  - Manage user accounts
  - Access system logs
  - Configure settings

### 4.2 Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us:

- **Cloud Storage Providers:**
  - Store photos and files securely

- **Analytics Services:**
  - App performance monitoring
  - Crash reporting

**Note:** All service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and can only use your information to perform services on our behalf.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

- Valid legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
- Government requests
- Protection of our rights and safety

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Data Storage

- **Server Location:** Your data is stored on secure servers
- **Backup:** Regular automated backups are performed
- **Retention:** Data is retained as long as your account is active or as needed for business purposes

### 5.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption:**
  - Data in transit: HTTPS/SSL encryption
  - Passwords: One-way hashing (bcrypt)
  - Sensitive data: Encrypted storage

- **Access Control:**
  - Role-based access permissions
  - Multi-factor authentication (when enabled)
  - Regular access audits

- **Security Practices:**
  - Regular security updates
  - Vulnerability assessments
  - Secure coding practices
  - Employee training on data protection

### 5.3 Data Breach

In the event of a data breach, we will:

1. Investigate the incident immediately
2. Notify affected users within 72 hours
3. Report to relevant authorities as required by law
4. Take corrective actions to prevent future breaches

### 6.4 Data Portability
